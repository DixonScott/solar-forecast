Solar Forecast UK

View project on GitHub

This app predicts solar panel efficiency (in kWh/kW) for the next 7 days based on your chosen location. Predictions are based on the weather forecast from Open-Meteo and a model trained on UK solar panel data from PVOutput.

Enter coordinates manually or click the map to choose a location. The average efficiency prediction error is 0.75kWh/kW (assuming the weather forecast is correct).

Optional: enter the power rating (in kW) of your solar panel system to get predictions of energy output.
